Local Fun
Amusements
Funtown Splashtown USA

Finally, for those hot summer days, nothing beats a trip to Funtown Splashtown USA. This family-friendly park offers a variety of thrilling water slides, pools, and amusement rides, ensuring a day full of smiles and laughter.

Palace Playland

A mere stone’s throw away is the Palace Playland, New England’s only beachfront amusement park. It features a blend of classic and modern rides, arcade games, and attractions for both kids and adults.

Pirate’s Cove Adventure Golf

Mini-golf enthusiasts have a wealth of options. Try Pirate’s Cove Adventure Golf, where you can putt away through a pirate-themed course, complete with animated characters, cascading waterfalls, and lush landscapes. For an even more immersive experience, the Jungle Adventure 3D Mini Golf offers an exotic rainforest-themed course with eye-popping 3D effects.

Dunegrass Golf Club

For a more traditional golfing experience, the Dunegrass Golf Club is the place to be. This 18-hole championship course is renowned for its scenic beauty and challenges suitable for all skill levels.
